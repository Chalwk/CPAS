Piper Archer IIIArcher II (PA-28-181)
Normal Checklist – MSFS 2020

EXTERIOR PREFLIGHT (WALK-AROUND)

Tie-downs / Chocks / Covers REMOVED
Fuel Quantity CHECK VISUALLY (Both tanks, drain samples)
Fuel Caps SECURED
Oil Level CHECK (6–8 quarts, min 5 quarts)
Oil Cap / Dipstick SECURED
Propeller CHECK (Condition, nicks, security)
Control Surfaces FREE & CORRECT (Ailerons, Elevator, Rudder, Flaps)
Pitot Tube Cover REMOVED
Static Ports CLEAR
Landing Gear CHECK (Tires, struts, condition)
Master Switch OFF during walk-around

COCKPIT PREPARATION

Parking Brake SET
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF
Throttle 1/2 INCH
Propeller FULL FORWARD (High RPM)
Carb Heat OFF
Avionics Master OFF
Master Switch ON
Fuel Selector BOTH (Verify detent)
Fuel Pump ON (Prime 2-3 seconds if cold)
Flaps UP (Electric switch)
Trim TAKEOFF (2-3 turns nose up from full down)
Circuit Breakers IN
Seat / Rudder Pedals ADJUSTED & LOCKED

ENGINE START

Area CLEAR
Mixture RICH
Throttle 1/4 INCH
Carb Heat OFF
Ignition START (Release at 800-1000 RPM)
Oil Pressure CHECK (Green arc: 60–90 psi within 30 sec)
Fuel Pump OFF after start
Alternator CHECK (Ammeter charging, low voltage light out)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT rising, Oil Temp 100–245°F)

RUN-UP AND MAG CHECK

Location RUN-UP AREA (Pointed into wind)
Parking Brake SET
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT
Throttle 1700–1800 RPM
Engine Instruments CHECK GREEN (Oil pressure 60–90 psi, Oil temp rising)
Magnetos CHECK (BOTH → L → BOTH → R → BOTH)
Max drop: 175 RPM
Difference L/R: < 50 RPM
Carb Heat CHECK (ON: expect RPM drop, then OFF)
Propeller Cycle CHECK (Reduce RPM to 1500, cycle prop 2-3 times)
RPM drop: 200–300 RPM
Vacuum / Suction CHECK (4.5–5.5 inHg)
Amps / Volts CHECK (Charging normal)
Avionics / Radios CHECK
Throttle 1000–1200 RPM (After checks)

BEFORE TAXI

Flaps UP (Verify)
Avionics Master ON
Transponder STANDBY
Radios / COM / NAV SET
Altimeter SET (Local field elevation)
Flight Instruments CHECK (AI, DG, VSI operational)
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT (Full deflection check)
Brakes TEST

TAXI

Taxi Light ON (As required)
Fuel Pump ON for taxi on rough ground or low fuel
Engine Gauges MONITOR (Oil temp/pressure in green)
Mixture AS REQUIRED (Lean for taxi above 1000 ft density altitude)
Propeller HIGH RPM (Full forward for taxi)
Nosewheel Steering RUDDER PEDALS (Differential braking if needed)

BEFORE TAKEOFF

Parking Brake SET
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT (Final check)
Flaps AS DESIRED (Typically 0–10° for normal takeoff)
Trim TAKEOFF (2-3 turns nose up)
Fuel Selector BOTH
Fuel Pump ON
Mixture FULL RICH
Carb Heat OFF
Propeller HIGH RPM (Full forward)
Flight Instruments SET (DG to compass)
Transponder ALT (Mode C)
Briefing TAKEOFF / DEPARTURE / EMERGENCY

TAKEOFF

Area CLEAR (Final runway check)
Flaps AS DESIRED
Fuel Pump ON
Throttle FULL (Smoothly to 2700 RPM)
Engine Instruments MONITOR (Oil pressure 60–90 psi, MP 25–27")
Airspeed ALIVE at 40 KIAS
Rotation 55–60 KIAS
Vy (Best Rate) 79 KIAS
Vx (Best Angle) 66 KIAS

CLIMB

Fuel Pump OFF (Above 500 ft AGL)
Flaps UP (If used, retract gradually)
Climb Speed 79–85 KIAS (Vy 79, cruise climb 85)
Propeller 2700 RPM (Full forward for climb)
Mixture LEAN (Above 3000 ft MSL for best power)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT ≤ 450°F, Oil Temp ≤ 245°F)

CRUISE

Power Setting 2300–2500 RPM (65–75% power)
22–24" MP
Propeller SET DESIRED RPM (Typically 2400-2500 for cruise)
Mixture LEAN (For peak EGT or 50°F rich of peak)
Cruise Speed 110–120 KIAS (75% power at 8000 ft)
Fuel Flow CHECK (9–10 GPH at 75% power)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT 300–400°F, Oil Temp 180–220°F ideal)
Fuel Tanks BALANCE (Switch tanks every 30-60 min if not using BOTH)

DESCENT

Power REDUCE (15–18" MP, 2000–2200 RPM)
Mixture RICH (Below 3000 ft MSL)
Carb Heat AS REQUIRED (ON when MP below 15")
Descent Speed 100–120 KIAS
Descent Rate 500–700 FPM
Engine Cooling MONITOR (Keep CHT > 250°F)
Fuel Pump ON (For landing)
Propeller HIGH RPM (Full forward)

BEFORE LANDING

Fuel Selector BOTH
Fuel Pump ON
Mixture FULL RICH
Carb Heat ON (Below 15" MP or green arc)
Propeller HIGH RPM (Full forward)
Seat Belts SECURED
Door LATCHED
Flaps AS DESIRED (Typically 10° on downwind, 25° on base, full on final)
Approach Speed 70–80 KIAS (70 with full flaps, 80 with no flaps)
Trim SET for approach speed

LANDING

Final Approach 70–75 KIAS (With flaps)
Flare GENTLY (Power to idle, hold off)
Touchdown MAIN WHEELS FIRST
After Touchdown NOSE WHEEL DOWN (Gently)
Flaps UP (After clearing runway)
Carb Heat OFF
Fuel Pump OFF

AFTER LANDING / TAXI-IN

Flaps UP
Transponder STANDBY
Lights AS REQUIRED
Propeller HIGH RPM (Full forward for taxi)
Engine Gauges MONITOR
Mixture LEAN for taxi if needed

ENGINE SHUTDOWN

Parking Brake SET
Avionics / Radios OFF
Fuel Pump OFF
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF (Smoothly pull to cutoff)
Ignition OFF after engine stops
Master Switch OFF

SECURING AIRCRAFT

Control Lock INSTALL
Pitot Cover INSTALL
Tie-downs SECURED
Fuel Selector OFF (If leaving aircraft)
Baggage / Documents REMOVED
Door LOCKED
