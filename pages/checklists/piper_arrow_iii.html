Piper Arrow III Checklist – MSFS 2020

IMPORTANT: This aircraft has retractable gear. Always verify 3 green lights before landing.

EXTERIOR PREFLIGHT (WALK-AROUND)

Tie-downs / Chocks / Covers REMOVED
Fuel Quantity CHECK VISUALLY (Drain samples from both wings, 50 gal total, 48 gal usable standard; 72 gal with tip tanks)
Fuel Caps SECURED
Oil Level CHECK (8 quarts, min 5 quarts, max 12 quarts)
Oil Cap / Dipstick SECURED
Landing Gear DOWN & VISUAL CHECK (Struts properly inflated, tires 24 psi mains, 36 psi nose)
Gear Doors CHECK (No damage, secure, no hydraulic leaks)
Control Surfaces FREE & CORRECT (Ailerons, Elevator, Rudder, Flaps, Trim tabs)
Pitot Tube Cover REMOVED
Static Ports CLEAR
Tires / Brakes CHECK (Condition / Inflation, tread depth > 1/16")
Propeller CHECK (No nicks, secure, spinner secure)
Exhaust System VISUAL CHECK (No cracks, secure mounting)
Master Switch OFF during walk-around

COCKPIT PREPARATION

Parking Brake SET
Battery / Alternator OFF
Avionics Master OFF
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF
Throttle 1/2 INCH
Propeller Control FULL FORWARD (High RPM)
Carb Heat OFF
Fuel Selector BOTH (Or fullest tank)
Fuel Pump OFF
Gear Lever DOWN (Verify 3 green lights during battery check)
Flaps UP
Trim TAKEOFF (Neutral or 2 divisions nose up)
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For ground ops)
Circuit Breakers IN
Seat / Rudder Pedals ADJUSTED & LOCKED
Shoulder Harnesses CHECK (Properly stowed)

ENGINE START

Area CLEAR (Prop area, shout "Clear!")
Master Switch ON (Check gear lights: 3 green)
Fuel Pump ON (Prime: Cold engine 4-6 seconds, Hot engine 1-2 seconds)
Mixture RICH
Throttle 1/4 INCH
Ignition START (Release at 800-1000 RPM, avoid continuous cranking > 30 sec)
Oil Pressure CHECK (Within 30 seconds, 25-100 psi, green arc 60-90 psi)
Fuel Pump OFF after start confirmed
Alternator ON / CHECK (Ammeter shows charge, low voltage light out)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT 150-400°F, Oil Temp 100-245°F)

RUN-UP AND SYSTEMS CHECK

Location RUN-UP AREA (Pointed into wind, 50 ft clear of obstructions)
Parking Brake SET
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT (Full deflection each direction)
Engine Instruments CHECK GREEN (Oil pressure 60-90 psi, Oil temp rising into green)
Propeller Control CYCLE (2300 RPM to 2000 RPM, check smooth RPM drop and recovery)
Magnetos CHECK (BOTH → L → BOTH → R → BOTH)
Max drop: 175 RPM
Difference L/R: < 50 RPM
No mag drop on BOTH
Carb Heat CHECK (ON: expect 75-125 RPM drop, then OFF)
Vacuum System CHECK (4.5-5.5 inHg for primary instruments)
Amps / Volts CHECK (Ammeter charging +10 to +60 amps, voltmeter 13.5-14.5V)
Fuel Flow CHECK (Steady, no fluctuations at run-up RPM)
Gear System CHECK (Warning horn when throttle reduced to idle with gear up)
Flaps OPERATE (Cycle to 25° and retract, check indicator)
Autopilot (if equipped) TEST (Turn on, verify disengage functions)
Stall Warning TEST (Should sound at flaps 0°, approx 65 KIAS)
Throttle 1000–1200 RPM (After checks)

BEFORE TAKEOFF

Parking Brake RELEASED
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT (Final check before entering runway)
Fuel Selector BOTH
Fuel Pump ON (For takeoff and landing)
Mixture FULL RICH (Below 3000 ft density altitude)
Propeller FULL FORWARD (High RPM, 2700)
Carb Heat OFF
Flaps AS DESIRED (0° normal, 25° short/soft field)
With flaps 25°: Rotate 5 KIAS slower, climb at 75 KIAS gear down
Trim TAKEOFF (2 divisions nose up from cruise)
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For takeoff and climb)
Avionics / Radios SET (COM/NAV frequencies, transponder ALT, autopilot OFF)
Altimeter SET (To current altimeter setting)
Directional Gyro SET (To magnetic compass, corrected for turning errors)
Clock START (Note takeoff time)
Doors / Windows CLOSED & LATCHED
Briefing TAKEOFF / DEPARTURE / EMERGENCY
Normal climb: 88 KIAS, Engine failure: Best glide 85 KIAS, Land straight ahead below 500'

TAKEOFF

Area CLEAR (Final runway check left and right)
Throttle FULL (Smoothly, monitor RPM: 2700, MP: 27-28" at sea level)
Engine Instruments MONITOR (Oil pressure 60-90 psi, CHT rising but < 400°F)
Airspeed ALIVE at 40 KIAS, rotate at 65-75 KIAS (flaps 0°)
Lift-off ESTABLISH POSITIVE RATE
Gear UP (Positive rate, 300-500 ft AGL, < 87 KIAS)
Vy (Best Rate) 88 KIAS (Gear up, flaps up)
Vx (Best Angle) 73 KIAS (Gear up, flaps up)
Climb Power 25" MP / 2500 RPM (75% power below 5000 ft)

CLIMB

Gear UP & VERIFIED (3 green lights out, gear handle UP)
Flaps UP (If used, retract at safe altitude and airspeed)
Fuel Pump OFF (Above 500 ft AGL, positive rate established)
Climb Power 25" MP / 2500 RPM (75% power up to 5000 ft)
MP decreases approx 1" per 1000 ft altitude gain
Climb Speed 88 KIAS (Vy) or 100 KIAS (cruise climb)
Cruise climb reduces noise and improves visibility
Mixture LEAN (Above 3000 ft for smooth operation)
Enrich if roughness occurs
Cowl Flaps AS REQUIRED (Adjust to maintain CHT 300-380°F)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT ≤ 400°F, Oil Temp ≤ 245°F, Oil pressure 60-90 psi)
Power Check AT CRUISE ALTITUDE (Set cruise power, then lean)

CRUISE

Power Setting 24" MP / 2400 RPM (65% power at 8000 ft)
23" MP / 2300 RPM for 55% power (economy cruise)
Mixture LEAN
Best power: 100°F rich of peak EGT
Best economy: 50°F lean of peak EGT
Always monitor for smooth operation
Cruise Speed 130 KTAS (65% power at 8000 ft)
120 KTAS at 55% power
Fuel Flow CHECK (10.5 GPH at 65% power, 9.0 GPH at 55% power)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT 300-380°F, Oil Temp 180-220°F ideal, Oil pressure 60-90 psi)
Cowl Flaps ADJUST (As needed for cooling, usually partially closed in cruise)
Fuel Tanks BALANCE (Switch tanks every 30 minutes if not using BOTH)
Performance Check EVERY HOUR (Fuel remaining vs planned, groundspeed check, ETA update)

DESCENT

Power REDUCE (15-18" MP, 2000-2200 RPM)
Mixture ENRICH (Below 5000 ft MSL for smooth operation)
Carb Heat AS REQUIRED (ON when RPM below green arc or in visible moisture)
Descent Speed 120-140 KIAS (With 500-1000 FPM descent)
Do not exceed 159 KIAS (Vne in smooth air)
Cowl Flaps CLOSED (To prevent overcooling during descent)
Engine Cooling MONITOR (Keep CHT > 250°F during descent to prevent shock cooling)
Limit CHT decrease to < 50°F per minute
Fuel Pump ON (Below 3000 ft AGL or when power < 15")
Gear Speed 140 KIAS MAX (Do not extend gear above this speed)
Before Landing Check START (When within 10 NM of destination or 3000 ft AGL)

BEFORE LANDING

Fuel Selector BOTH (Or fullest tank)
Fuel Pump ON (Below 3000 ft AGL)
Mixture FULL RICH (Below 3000 ft MSL)
Propeller FULL FORWARD (High RPM)
Carb Heat ON (Below green arc, typically < 2000 RPM)
Gear DOWN (Below 140 KIAS, verify 3 green lights)
Gear warning horn will sound if throttle reduced with gear up
Gear Horn Check VERIFY (Reduce throttle to idle briefly, horn should sound if gear not down)
Seat Belts SECURED (All occupants)
Shoulder Harnesses SECURED
Flaps AS DESIRED
10° on downwind: +5 KIAS
25° on base: +0 KIAS
40° on final: -5 KIAS
Approach Speed 75-85 KIAS
Flaps 0°: 85 KIAS
Flaps 25°: 80 KIAS
Flaps 40°: 75 KIAS
Add 1/2 gust factor
Trim SET for approach speed
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For landing)
Landing Light ON (If equipped, below 10,000 ft and within 10 NM of airport)

LANDING

Final Approach 75 KIAS (With full flaps, adjust for conditions)
Threshold Crossing 70 KIAS (50 ft over threshold)
Flare GENTLY (Power to idle at 10-20 ft, gradual back pressure)
Touchdown MAIN WHEELS FIRST (Airspeed 60-65 KIAS)
After Touchdown NOSE WHEEL DOWN (Gently, after aerodynamic braking)
Hold full back pressure during rollout
Flaps UP (After clearing runway, improves braking)
Carb Heat OFF
Braking AS REQUIRED (Differential braking for steering)

AFTER LANDING / TAXI-IN

Flaps UP
Transponder STANDBY
Landing Light OFF (If equipped)
Fuel Pump OFF (Unless low fuel or rough ground)
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For ground cooling)
Mixture LEAN for taxi if needed (Above 2000 ft density altitude)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (During taxi)
Taxi Speed WALKING PACE (Prop blast consideration)
Parking INTO WIND (If possible)

ENGINE SHUTDOWN

Parking Brake SET
Avionics / Radios OFF
Fuel Pump OFF
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF (Smoothly pull to full lean)
Ignition OFF (After engine stops)
Master Switch OFF
Cowl Flaps CLOSED
Control Lock INSTALL (If available)

SECURING AIRCRAFT

Control Lock INSTALL (Prevents wind damage)
Pitot Cover INSTALL
Static Port Covers INSTALL (If available)
Tie-downs SECURED (3-point minimum: nose, each wing)
Chocks INSTALLED (Front of main wheels)
Fuel Selector OFF
Baggage / Documents REMOVED
Door LOCKED
Logbook Entry COMPLETE (Hobbs/Tach times, discrepancies)
