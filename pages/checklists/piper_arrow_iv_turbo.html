PA-28R-201T Piper Turbo Arrow IV
Normal Checklist – MSFS 2020

EXTERIOR PREFLIGHT (WALK-AROUND)

Tie-downs / Chocks / Covers REMOVED
Fuel Quantity CHECK VISUALLY (Drain samples from both wings, 72 gal total, 68 gal usable)
Fuel Caps SECURED
Oil Level CHECK (8 quarts, min 6 quarts, max 12 quarts)
Oil Cap / Dipstick SECURED
Gear & Doors VISUAL CHECK (No damage, secure)
Control Surfaces FREE & CORRECT (Ailerons, Elevator, Rudder, Flaps, Trim tabs)
Pitot Tube Cover REMOVED
Static Ports CLEAR
Tires / Brakes CHECK (Condition / Inflation, 24 psi mains, 36 psi nose)
Propeller CHECK (No nicks, secure)
Turbocharger / Exhaust VISUAL CHECK (No visible damage or leaks)
Master Switch OFF during walk-around

COCKPIT PREPARATION

Parking Brake SET
Battery / Alternator OFF
Avionics Master OFF
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF
Throttle 1/2 INCH
Propeller Control FULL FORWARD (High RPM)
Carb Heat OFF
Fuel Selector BOTH (Or fullest tank)
Fuel Pump OFF
Gear Lever DOWN (Verify 3 green lights during battery check)
Flaps UP
Trim TAKEOFF (Neutral or slightly nose up)
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For ground ops)
Circuit Breakers IN
Seat / Rudder Pedals ADJUSTED & LOCKED

ENGINE START

Area CLEAR
Master Switch ON (Check gear lights: 3 green)
Fuel Pump ON (Prime 3-5 seconds cold, 1-2 seconds hot)
Mixture RICH
Throttle 1/4 INCH
Ignition START (Release at 800-1000 RPM)
Oil Pressure CHECK (Within 30 seconds, 25-100 psi)
Fuel Pump OFF after start confirmed
Alternator ON / CHECK (Ammeter charging)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT 150-400°F, Oil Temp 100-245°F)

RUN-UP AND SYSTEMS CHECK

Location RUN-UP AREA (Pointed into wind)
Parking Brake SET
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT
Engine Instruments CHECK GREEN (Oil pressure 25-100 psi, Oil temp rising)
Propeller Control CYCLE (2300 RPM to 2000 RPM, check RPM drop)
Magnetos CHECK (BOTH → L → BOTH → R → BOTH)
Max drop: 150 RPM
Difference L/R: < 50 RPM
Carb Heat CHECK (ON: expect 75-125 RPM drop)
Vacuum System CHECK (4.5-5.5 inHg)
Amps / Volts CHECK (Ammeter charging, voltmeter 13.5-14.5V)
Fuel Flow CHECK (Steady)
Gear System CHECK (Warning horn when throttle reduced with gear up)
Flaps OPERATE (Cycle to 25° and retract)
Throttle 1000–1200 RPM (After checks)

BEFORE TAKEOFF

Parking Brake RELEASED
Flight Controls FREE & CORRECT (Final check)
Fuel Selector BOTH
Fuel Pump ON (For takeoff and landing)
Mixture FULL RICH (Below 75% power settings)
Propeller FULL FORWARD (High RPM, 2700)
Carb Heat OFF
Flaps AS DESIRED (0° normal, 25° short/soft field)
Trim TAKEOFF (Neutral)
Cowl Flaps OPEN
Avionics / Radios SET
Transponder ALT
Briefing TAKEOFF / DEPARTURE / EMERGENCY

TAKEOFF

Area CLEAR (Final runway check)
Throttle FULL (Smoothly, monitor MP: 36-38" at sea level)
Engine Instruments MONITOR (Oil pressure 25-100 psi, CHT rising)
Airspeed ALIVE at 50 KIAS, rotate at 65-75 KIAS
Gear UP (Positive rate, 300-500 ft AGL)
Vy (Best Rate) 88 KIAS (Gear up, flaps up)
Vx (Best Angle) 73 KIAS (Gear up, flaps up)

CLIMB

Gear UP & VERIFIED (3 green lights out)
Flaps UP (If used)
Fuel Pump OFF (Above 500 ft AGL, positive rate)
Climb Power 29" MP / 2500 RPM (75% power below 10,000 ft)
Climb Speed 100 KIAS (Normal climb, gear up)
Mixture LEAN (Above 5000 ft for best power)
Cowl Flaps AS REQUIRED (Adjust to maintain CHT ≤ 400°F)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT ≤ 400°F, Oil Temp ≤ 245°F)

CRUISE

Power Setting 24-27" MP / 2300-2500 RPM (65-75% power)
Turbocharged: Maintain MP with altitude (approx 1" loss per 1000 ft without turbo)
Mixture LEAN (For peak EGT or 50°F rich of peak)
Cruise Speed 140-155 KIAS (75% power at 10,000 ft)
Fuel Flow CHECK (12-14 GPH at 75% power)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (CHT 300-380°F, Oil Temp 180-220°F ideal)
Cowl Flaps ADJUST (As needed for cooling)
Fuel Tanks BALANCE (Switch tanks every 30-60 minutes)

DESCENT

Power REDUCE (15-18" MP, 2000-2200 RPM)
Mixture ENRICH (As required for smooth operation)
Carb Heat AS REQUIRED (ON when below green arc or visible moisture)
Descent Speed 120-140 KIAS (With 500-1000 FPM descent)
Cowl Flaps CLOSED (To prevent overcooling)
Engine Cooling MONITOR (Keep CHT > 250°F during descent)
Fuel Pump ON (Below 3000 ft AGL or when power < 15")

BEFORE LANDING

Fuel Selector BOTH (Or fullest tank)
Fuel Pump ON
Mixture FULL RICH (Below 5000 ft MSL)
Propeller FULL FORWARD (High RPM)
Carb Heat ON (Below green arc)
Gear DOWN (Below 140 KIAS, verify 3 green)
Gear Horn CHECK (Power to idle should trigger horn if gear not down)
Seat Belts SECURED
Flaps AS DESIRED (10° on downwind, 25° on base, 40° on final)
Approach Speed 75-85 KIAS (75 with flaps 40°, 85 with flaps 0°)
Trim SET for approach speed

LANDING

Final Approach 70-75 KIAS (With full flaps)
Flare GENTLY (Power to idle, hold off)
Touchdown MAIN WHEELS FIRST
After Touchdown NOSE WHEEL DOWN (Gently)
Flaps UP (After clearing runway)
Carb Heat OFF

AFTER LANDING / TAXI-IN

Flaps UP
Transponder STANDBY
Fuel Pump OFF (Unless low fuel or rough ground)
Cowl Flaps OPEN (For ground cooling)
Engine Gauges MONITOR (During taxi)
Mixture LEAN for taxi if needed

ENGINE SHUTDOWN

Parking Brake SET
Avionics / Radios OFF
Fuel Pump OFF
Mixture IDLE CUTOFF
Ignition OFF (After engine stops)
Master Switch OFF
Cowl Flaps CLOSED

SECURING AIRCRAFT

Control Lock INSTALL
Pitot Cover INSTALL
Tie-downs SECURED (3-point minimum)
Fuel Selector OFF
Baggage / Documents REMOVED
Door LOCKED
