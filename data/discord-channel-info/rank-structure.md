# CPAS Pilot Rank Structure

We operate with a structured rank system designed for progressive skill development and operational responsibility.

## **Operational Ranks**

### @Pilot Under Training

* Initial training phase for newly accepted pilots
* Access to training aircraft only
* Requires instructor oversight on all flights

### @Charter Pilot

* Basic training complete
* Can fly simple charter routes solo
* Access to basic fleet (Warrior, R44)

### @Senior Charter Pilot

* Proven charter experience
* Access to entire fleet (except H145)
* Can fly complex/mountain routes

### @Lead Pilot

* Extensive charter & mountain experience
* Full fleet access including H145
* Can approve new pilots for solo

### @Instructor

* Exceptional knowledge & teaching ability
* Conducts check rides and training
* Maintains training standards

## **Administrative Roles**

### @Admin

* Overall server management and leadership
* Final authority on community decisions
* Owner/Leader of Coastal Peaks Air Service

**Note:** Detailed rank requirements, privileges, and progression criteria can be found in
our [Training Structure](https://chalwk.github.io/CPAS/pages/training_structure.html).
