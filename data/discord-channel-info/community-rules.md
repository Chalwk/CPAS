## TL;DR - Key Community Expectations
1. **Be Respectful** - Treat everyone with courtesy and professionalism
2. **Fly Realistically** - Follow proper procedures for immersion
3. **Help Others** - Share knowledge and support fellow pilots
4. **Keep it Fun** - Remember this is virtual aviation for enjoyment
5. **Stay on Topic** - Use appropriate channels for discussions
