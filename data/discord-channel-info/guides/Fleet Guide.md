**:airplane: CPAS Virtual Fleet Overview**

Our fleet features high-quality MSFS add-ons for realistic charter operations.

**Helicopters:**

- **R44** (Free) - Training, light charters
- **R66** (Payware) - Extended range, turbine
- **H125** (Payware) - Mountain/utility operations
- **H145** (Payware) - Executive/SAR operations

**Fixed-Wing:**

- **Piper Tomahawk** - Primary training
- **Piper Warrior II** - Basic charters/training
- **Piper Archer II** - Comfortable passenger shuttle
- **Piper Arrow III/IV** - Complex operations, advanced charters

**Key Points:**

- Each aircraft has detailed checklists on our website
- SimBrief airframe configurations available for most aircraft
- Start with basics (R44 or Warrior) and progress as you rank up
- Custom liveries encouraged!
